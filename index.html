FAQ

GOOD QUESTIONS.
STRAIGHT ANSWERS.

Do I need to be home? +

Nope. As long as your bin is at the curb after your trash pickup, we'll clean it and send you a before/after photo when we're done. You don't need to lift a finger.

What day will you come? +

We assign you a service day based on your neighborhood. Southeast Queens is Monday, Jamaica/St. Albans is Tuesday, Ozone Park/Howard Beach is Wednesday, Forest Hills/Elmhurst is Thursday, Flushing/Bayside is Friday, and Astoria/LIC/Rockaways is Saturday. You'll always know your day in advance.

How does the $10 extra bin fee work? +

Your first bin is $25 (one-time) or $18/month. Each additional bin at your property — trash, recycling, compost — is just $10 per clean. So two bins one-time is $35. Two bins monthly is $28/month. Simple.

What exactly do you clean with? +

We use 200°F high-pressure hot water that eliminates 99.9% of bacteria including E. coli, Salmonella, and Listeria. We follow with a biodegradable eco-friendly deodorizer. All dirty water is contained in our unit — nothing runs into your driveway or the storm drain.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no nonsense. Text or email us and we'll cancel or pause your plan same day. We'd rather earn your loyalty every month than lock you in.

Do you serve my neighborhood? +

We serve all of Queens — every ZIP code. From Springfield Gardens to Astoria, Howard Beach to Whitestone. If you're in Queens, you're in our service area. Book and we'll confirm your route day within 24 hours.
